Solo traveller tips for Sanur
Sanur ≠ Seminyak — no aggressive clubs or high-pressure bar touts. It's laid-back, walkable and genuinely solo-friendly.
Happy hour 4–7pm is the sweet spot — mix of solo travellers, expats, couples easing into the evening. Soul on the Beach, SBG, Shotgun Social.
Live music from 8:30–9pm is when bars get social. Casablanca has billiards too — good icebreaker activity alone.
Best bars to meet people: Sanur Beer Garden (communal tables) and Lola's (tiny bar, regulars chat freely).
For a quieter solo night: GORO GORO for jazz + food, Lia's for a calm drink, La Playa for solo sunset dinner with a view.
Night plan

Solo sunset & late drinks in Sanur

Sanur is better for laid-back bars than true all-night beach clubs. This is the clean version of the route.

Best solo sunset
Tree Bar — the nicest solo sunset drink in Sanur. Beachfront, calmer, easy to sit with one drink and not feel awkward.
Beach-club alternative
Byrd House — prettier, more scene, still fine solo, better if you want sunset with a bit more energy.
Late option
Casablanca — the most reliable proper late-night Sanur move, usually the easiest place to keep the night going.
After-hours only if needed
Lim Ling — the messy after-hours fallback, not the polished beach-club version.
Recommended route
17:30–19:00 Tree Bar for sunset · 19:30–21:00 Jalapeño for dinner · 21:30 onwards Casablanca only if you still want one more spot.
