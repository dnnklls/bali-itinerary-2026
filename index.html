How to use this
Think in layers, not checklists
The only fixed pieces are your Sanur base and one board-sport morning. Everything else is modular. If you wake up lazy, do Sanur. If your energy is good, take one clean outing to Jimbaran, Uluwatu, or Ubud, then come back.
Main route

The easiest version of this trip

Day 1 · Arrival
Check into Sanur and do almost nothing
Get into your hotel, walk the beach path, have an easy first meal, and let Bali stay simple on the first day. Good first-night options: Tandjung Sari Restaurant, Massimo, or Byrd House.
Maps · promenade Dinner · Massimo
Day 2 · Morning anchor
Do a Sanur surf lesson
This is the clean version of the trip. Do a beginner-friendly Sanur surf session with a local school, keep the rest of the day loose, then come back for a massage and an easy dinner.
Maps · surf school Official pricing
Day 3 · Soft reset
Sanur sunrise, brunch, beach, repeat
Do Karang Beach or Sindhu early, brunch at Sala Bistro or Genius Cafe, then keep the middle of the day empty on purpose. This should feel like leave, not output.
Maps · Karang Brunch · Sala
Day 4 · Optional south Bali
Pick one clean outing: Jimbaran or Uluwatu
If you want one proper outing, make it south Bali. Jimbaran is easier and softer. Uluwatu gives you the big scenic hit. Do not try to cram both with a dozen other stops.
Sunset · Jimbaran Scenic option · Uluwatu
Day 5 · Optional culture day
If you want one inland day, do a light Ubud sampler
Go early, keep it to Monkey Forest plus a central Ubud stroll, then come back. It is there to change the texture of the trip a little, not to become a second holiday inside the holiday.
Maps · Monkey Forest Lower-effort swap · Seminyak lunch
Day 6 · Free buffer
Leave one day deliberately underbooked
Use it for a second surf lesson, a Sanur Lagoon wakeboard session, a long massage, beach time, a lazy lunch, or absolutely nothing. This is the day that stops the trip from feeling too planned.
Maps · Mertasari Optional second session
Departure day
Slow breakfast, pack, and leave cleanly
No dramatic final sprint. One last café, one last walk, one last coffee, then airport whenever you need it.
