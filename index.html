Maya Sanur Resort & SpaPicked stay
This is the corrected base for the narrowed-down plan. Public pricing snapshots show it from about US$186. Roughly 1.9 km from the surf school and about 15 km from DPS airport, usually around 25 to 35 minutes by car.
InterContinental Bali Sanur ResortClosest luxe pick
Closest true luxury option to the surf side, and the nicest location match if you want Mertasari energy. Roughly 1.1 km from the surf school and about 14.4 km from DPS airport. Catch is price, public snapshots put it around US$430+, so this is the stretch option.
Andaz BaliBest if budget bends
Probably the prettiest 5-star stay in this zone, but usually not the value play. Public snapshots show it around €208 / US$220-ish. Roughly 2.5 km from the surf school and about 14 to 15 km from DPS airport.
